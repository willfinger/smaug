Humanizer is a Claude Code skill that removes signs of AI-generated writing from text, making AI content sound more natural and human-like. This tool leverages Wikipedia's comprehensive list of "signs of AI writing" to analyze and modify text to avoid robotic-sounding patterns and produce more authentic content.

## Key Features

- **AI Pattern Detection**: Identifies and removes common AI writing patterns identified by Wikipedia
- **Text Humanization**: Transforms AI-generated text to sound more natural and human
- **Comprehensive Coverage**: Addresses multiple AI writing issues including robotic tone, formulaic patterns, and unnatural phrasing
- **Claude Code Integration**: Seamlessly integrated as a Claude Code skill for easy use
- **Flexible Configuration**: Customizable settings for different writing styles and use cases
- **Batch Processing**: Capable of processing multiple texts at once for efficiency
- **Real-time Analysis**: Provides immediate feedback on text modifications
- **Cross-Platform**: Works with various Claude models and platforms
- **Lightweight Design**: Minimal resource requirements for easy deployment
- **Extensible Framework**: Allows adding new AI writing patterns and improvement rules

## Use Cases

- Enhancing AI-generated content to sound more natural and human
- Improving chatbot responses to feel more conversational
- Creating more engaging content marketing materials
- Reducing detectability of AI-written text
- Improving the quality of AI-assisted writing
- Creating more authentic social media posts
- Developing better email marketing copy
- Enhancing AI-powered customer support responses
- Creating more natural-sounding dialogue for applications
- Improving the readability of AI-generated technical documentation
