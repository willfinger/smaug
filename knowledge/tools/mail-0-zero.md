Zero is an open-source AI email solution that gives users the power to self-host their own email app while also integrating external services like Gmail and other email providers. It's designed to modernize and improve email through AI agents with a focus on privacy, self-hosting, and a unified inbox experience.

## Key Features

- **Open-Source** - Fully transparent with no hidden agendas
- **AI Driven** - Enhance emails with Agents & LLMs for intelligent processing
- **Data Privacy First** - No tracking, collection, or selling of user data
- **Self-Hosting Freedom** - Easy to run your own email app locally
- **Unified Inbox** - Connect multiple email providers like Gmail, Outlook, and more
- **Customizable UI & Features** - Tailor the email experience to your preferences
- **Developer-Friendly** - Built with extensibility and integrations in mind
- **AI Agents Integration** - Leverage AI for email automation and enhancement

## Use Cases

- Private email hosting with complete control over your data
- Power users who want to integrate multiple email accounts
- Developers looking for a customizable email platform
- Teams needing a self-hosted email solution with AI capabilities
- Privacy-conscious individuals who want to avoid data-hungry email services
- Email automation and enhancement through AI agents
