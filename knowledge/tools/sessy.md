Sessy is an open-source email observability platform specifically designed for AWS SES that provides a beautiful, self-hosted interface to monitor and analyze email delivery, bounces, complaints, opens, clicks, and other events without relying on expensive commercial SES wrappers. Created by Marc KÃ¶hlbrugge, Sessy emerged from his experience switching from Postmark to AWS SES, offering an affordable, open-source alternative to the VC-backed email monitoring services.

## Key Features

- **Event Timeline** - Single unified view of all email events including sends, deliveries, bounces, complaints, opens, and clicks
- **Real-time Monitoring** - Track email performance and deliverability metrics in real-time
- **Self-Hosted** - Full control over data and infrastructure - no SaaS vendor lock-in
- **Cost-Effective** - Eliminate expensive monthly SES wrapper fees
- **Beautiful UI** - Professional, polished interface for comprehensive email monitoring
- **Open Source** - O'Saasy licensed with commercial-friendly terms
- **Docker Support** - Easy deployment with containerized images
- **AWS SES Integration** - Deep integration with Amazon SES event notifications

## Use Cases

- **Email Deliverability Monitoring** - Track bounce rates, spam complaints, and deliverability health
- **Email Engagement Analytics** - Monitor open rates, click-through rates, and user engagement
- **Cost Optimization** - Replace expensive email monitoring services like Postmark or Mailgun
- **Privacy Compliance** - Self-hosted solution for organizations with strict data privacy requirements
- **Development Teams** - Professional email monitoring for development environments
- **E-commerce Platforms** - Monitor transactional emails, marketing campaigns, and user communications
